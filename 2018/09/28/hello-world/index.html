Biography

I am currently a master student at Human Cyber Physical Intelligence Integration Lab in Sun Yat-Sen University advised by Prof. Liang Lin. I received my Bachelor Degree in the School of Data and Computer Science, Sun Yat-sen University in 2016.
Email: kegong936 AT gmail.com

Research Interest

My general research interest is to develop interpretable and structured machine learning techniques for computer vision tasks. I mainly investigate to exploit human commonsense and domain knowledge and further incorporate them to develop the advanced artificial intelligence system.

  • Human-centric Analysis, including human pose estimation, human parsing
  • Object understanding, including classification, segmentation, detection
  • Speech recognition and synthesis.
